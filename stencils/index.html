about Airi - aka Gabrielle Iskandar

Creative designer, art director, front-end developer, visual communicator, service designer.

Designs websites, user interfaces, brands, logos, icon sets. Passionate about typography, and happy to lose oneself in a sketch.

Co-founder of Omnijar Studio; a digital design, development, and protoptyping studio, specializing in bleeding edge technologies, and the sweet spot where articifical intelligence meets user experience.

I design

user interfaces for the web, progressive web apps, and native applications. Crafting full projects from inception to production, including UX and branding.

Branding, icon sets, color palettes, and typography play a large supporting role in the quality of the finished product and the message communicated.

UI designs are based on solid user experience patterns and human-centered design thinking. All concepts are created to be built, based on a knowledge of technology and possibility.

I build

user interfaces, be it a landing page, administrative frontend, or custom web application.

Starting with a semantically apporopriate, clean, and logical structure, with progressively enhanced, modern, and cascading styling. Occasionally – during build or testing – part of the design gets a little update and rework.

Responsivity and accessibility are key factors in a successful project, code is modular and reasoned allowing for easy management, readability, and future updates and improvements.

I improve

projects through an iterative process, backed by an up-to-date knowledge of current web standards.

Cleaning up, stripping down, improving, iterating, and enhancing (design and build) projects of my own and others is a passion. Improving the output, even when it's invisible to most, and reinforcing the core message.

Sometimes to truly improve the project – look, feel, or flow – requires a blank page and a fresh mind, which takes us full-circle back to the design.
